About Me

I am a climate scientist at the French National Research Institute for Sustainable Development (IRD). My expertise includes climate dynamics, large-scale atmospheric teleconnections and climate impacts on health. In my research, I have focused on extreme climate events (heatwaves, droughts), tropical and subtropical precipitation changes, large-scale atmospheric teleconnections as well as impacts of heat on mortality and occupational health.

Recent projects

  • TipESM - Exploring Tipping Points and Their Impacts Using Earth System Models (funded by EU through Horizon Europe)
  • StewARD - STatistical Early WArning systems of weather-related Risks from probabilistic forecasts, over cities in West Africa (funded by ANR, France)
  • ArcIce4MedRain - Impacts of Arctic Sea Ice Loss on Future Rainfall Changes in Mediterranean Region (funded by La Caixa Foundation, Spain)
